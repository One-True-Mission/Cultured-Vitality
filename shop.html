How the protocol works

Three months, three steps.

Most people complete a 90-day cycle. Here's what to expect at each stage so you know exactly how to dose, and when to step up.

01

Month one: the starter jar

Begin with 1/4 teaspoon twice daily. The dose is small on purpose. The microbes in Cultured Vitality are potent, and a slow ramp lets your body detox gently instead of all at once.

02

Month two: the full jar

Move to one tablespoon twice daily. This is the standard dose, and one full liter jar covers the full month. Most people start to notice changes in sleep, digestion, and energy in this window.

03

Month three: full reset

Continue at one tablespoon twice daily. By the end of month three, the cultures have had time to truly take hold in your gut microbiome, which is where the deeper, longer-lasting changes show up.
